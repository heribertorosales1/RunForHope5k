A Run for Awareness, Hope, and Community

Run for Hope 5K is a student-led philanthropy event hosted by the brothers of Pi Kappa Alpha and Pi Kappa Phi at the University of Alabama.

All proceeds support Active Minds, the nation’s leading nonprofit dedicated to mental health awareness, education, and suicide prevention among students and young adults.

FAQ

Can I walk instead of run?

Yes — walkers are welcome.

Is the event rain or shine?

Yes, unless conditions are unsafe.

Where does the money go?

Registration fees help fund the event and our contribution to Active Minds. Direct donations to Active Minds are tax-deductible.
